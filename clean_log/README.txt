clean_log.pl:

Usage:
perl clean_log.pl

Information:
In this script, there are tow parameter:
my $RM_INVERVAL=30;
my $GZIP_INVERVAL=5;

RM_INVERVAL: Remove the log files before xx days
GZIP_INVERVAL: Use gzip to compress the log files before xx days

You can replace this two parameters under your requirement.
